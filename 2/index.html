For most Americans, their home is the most valuable single asset they’ll ever own. And yet, over 90% of the population has never had their home appraised—aside from the initial appraisal at the time of purchase.

But did you know that a home appraisal can…

  • Help you get your home re-financed?

Most homeowners don’t know that the need for appraisals doesn’t end the minute you buy a home.

In fact, getting a valuation is all the more important the longer you own your home.
